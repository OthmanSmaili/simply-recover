About Simply Recover OC

Guided healing with clarity and compassion

Simply Recover OC is an accredited, state-of-the-art rehabilitation center located in the heart of Orange County. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional, evidence-based care for individuals struggling with mental health challenges, trauma, depression, substance abuse, and related conditions.

Our Mission

To make the path to healing clear, accessible, and compassionate for every person and family we serve.

Our Programs

Structured treatment options tailored to your needs

Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP)

Our PHP offers full-day structured therapeutic support, perfect for those who need intensive care without overnight stays.

Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

Our IOP provides flexible, part-time treatment, allowing clients to balance work, school, and daily life while focusing on recovery.
